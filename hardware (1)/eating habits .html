Disease Prevention: A nutritious diet protects against noncommunicable diseases such as obesity, heart disease, stroke, and diabetes.

Physical Health & Strength: It strengthens bones, supports muscles, and promotes healthy skin and teeth.

  1. Energy and Immunity: Good nutrition ensures higher energy levels throughout the day and boosts the immune system.
  2. Weight Management: It helps achieve and maintain a healthy weight.
  3. Mental Well-being: Balanced, nutrient-dense foods support brain function, improve mood, and can lead to better sleep.

Variety and Balance: Eating a wide range of foods, including plenty of fruits, vegetables, and whole grains.

Chronic Illness: It significantly reduces the risk of heart disease, stroke, type 2 diabetes, and various cancers, particularly bowel cancer.
