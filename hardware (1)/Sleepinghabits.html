sleeping habits

Hormone Regulation: A routine ensures the timely release of melatonin to help you fall asleep and cortisol to help you wake up.

Predictability: Consistency "trains" your brain to recognize bedtime cues, reducing the time spent tossing and turning (sleep onset latency).
