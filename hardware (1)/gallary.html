What Does a gallary Do?

Dopamine Boost: Seeing physical evidence of hard work triggers a sense of accomplishment and a "dopamine hit," which fuels the motivation to maintain consistency..

Overcoming Plateaus: During periods when the scale doesn't move (plateaus), looking back at "Day 1" photos can reignite drive and remind you how far you have already come.

What Are GPUs Used For?

Increased Accountability: Committing to a regular photo schedule creates a "meaningful ritual" of self-documentation, making you more likely to stick to your goals.
